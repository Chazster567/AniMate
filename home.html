Digital Culture

My work on Digital Culture includes a video exploring the history of Virtual Reality and an audio work exploring the shift from analogue to digital. These pieces were created to test my video and audio editing abilities. The video covers the origin of VR and early forms of immersion and then progresses into more recent ventures into this emerging media format. The audio impersonates the feel of a vinyl record being played and toys with the idea of tricking the listener.
